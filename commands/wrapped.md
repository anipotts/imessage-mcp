## What This Does

Generates a Spotify Wrapped-style year-in-review for your iMessage activity. Shows total message counts, top contacts, busiest months, texting personality insights, and standout moments from any calendar year.

## How To Use

Ask for your wrapped for any year. If you do not specify a year, it defaults to the previous calendar year. You can also scope it to a specific group chat or compare multiple years side by side.

## Tools Orchestrated

1. `yearly_wrapped` -- Generates the full year-in-review dataset for the requested year

## Examples

- "Give me my 2024 wrapped"
- "Compare my 2023 vs 2024 texting"
- "Wrapped for just my family group chat"
- "How did my texting habits change from 2022 to 2024?"

## Tips

- Defaults to the previous calendar year if no year is specified
- Use emoji section headers for a fun, visual presentation
- Ask for a comparison across years to spot trends in your texting behavior
- Works great as a shareable summary -- ask for a condensed version you can screenshot
