## What This Does

Surfaces messages from the same date in previous years -- a time capsule of what you were texting about on this day in the past. Also finds the very first and last messages exchanged with any contact for milestone moments.

## How To Use

Ask about a specific date, or just say "on this day" to see what you were texting about on today's date in prior years. You can scope it to a specific contact or see everything across all conversations.

## Tools Orchestrated

1. `on_this_day` -- Retrieves messages from the current date across all previous years
2. `first_last_message` -- Finds the very first and most recent messages with a contact

## Examples

- "What was I texting about on this day last year?"
- "Show me memories with Mom"
- "What was the first text I ever sent my best friend?"
- "On this day in 2021 -- what was going on?"

## Tips

- Great for a daily check-in routine -- run it every morning for a nostalgia hit
- Scope to a specific contact for more meaningful, focused memories
- Combine with `first_last_message` to bookend a relationship -- see how it started and where it is now
- Works well as a conversation starter -- share the results with the person you were texting
